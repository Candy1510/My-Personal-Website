About Me

Interests and Hobbies

I have a wide range of interests and hobbies that enrich both my personal and professional life. Spending time with family, especially my four wonderful grandchildren, is one of my greatest joys and keeps me deeply grounded. I also have a passion for creative pursuits, including arts and crafts such as painting, jewelry making, and sewing. These activities not only allow me to express myself but also help me maintain attention to detail and creativity. In addition, I enjoy cooking, singing, dancing, and writing, particularly journaling and poetry, as a way to nurture self-expression and emotional well-being. I value experiences that broaden my perspectives, whether it is attending cultural events like movies, concerts, and plays, or traveling to explore new places. My curiosity also extends to learning and technology, where I am continually seeking opportunities to expand my knowledge and embrace innovation. These passions fuel my creativity, strengthen my problem-solving skills, and foster meaningful connections—all of which enhance my ability to contribute effectively in any setting.

My Passion

I am passionate about supporting individuals and families affected by mental illness, addiction, and homelessness. My dedication stems from personal experience, having faced and overcome similar challenges in my own life. Through that journey, I gained valuable insights into the systemic barriers many encounter and the resources that can truly make a difference. My ultimate goal is to leverage technology to empower others by creating apps and websites that provide accessible tools and guidance. These platforms will be designed to help individuals and their families navigate available services, recover from difficult circumstances, and achieve sustainable self-sufficiency. I am committed to continuously honing my skills and knowledge in technology and development to bring this vision to life and positively impact those in need.

My Inspiration

A significant source of my inspiration is my mother, whose remarkable perseverance left a lasting impression on me. While raising my siblings and me, she pursued her education and became a successful programmer, exemplifying determination and the transformative power of technology. Her journey ignited my interest in technology and demonstrated its potential to drive impactful solutions. I aim to channel my love for learning, creativity, and technology into initiatives that help individuals and families navigate life’s challenges. By developing innovative tools and platforms, my goal is to empower others to overcome obstacles, achieve self-sufficiency, and thrive.
